## 1. Abstract
The Volition Protocol introduces a novel **Multi-Agent Context-Sharded Consensus (MACSC)** framework. Originally conceived out of pure necessity during the development of GAP (Guardian Angel Protocol), it bypasses traditional peer-review networks by utilizing a **Synthetic Intelligence Think-Tank**. This is an **Architect-Governed Recursive Forge** where a human architect and a persistent **Partner-in-State** utilize parallel AI audits to hammer raw concepts into hardened logic through a discretionary, collaborative feedback loop.

## 2. The 4-Phase Workflow
The protocol follows a linear start with a **User-Discretionary Recursive Core (Phases 2-3)** that continues only until the Human Architect grants the "Green Light" for Phase 4.

### Phase 1: Inception (The Anchor - One-Time Setup)
The Architect and the **Primary State-Controller (PSC)** establish the baseline: project intent, foundational logic, and technical constraints (e.g., ARMv9-A CCA). This sets the "North Star" for the entire cycle.

### Phase 2: The Forge (Parallel or Synced Audits - RECURSIVE)
The draft is broadcast to a pool of **Adversarial Nodes (SANs)**.
- **Coverage Sync:** Toggled between **Stealth** (Blind audits to prevent shared hallucinations) or **Synced** (Collaborative audits to eliminate redundancy and find "Negative Space").
- **Architect-Governed Depth:** The user dictates the quantity and range of the AI Viewboard.

### Phase 3: The Workshop (Human-PSC Partnership - RECURSIVE)
This is the core evaluative cycle. The Architect and the **PSC (Partner-in-State)** sift through the Forge results in a 5-step "Heat Cycle":

1.  **The Broadcast:** Metadata-tagged critiques are gathered from the Forge nodes.
2.  **The PSC Audit:** The PSC scans the mess and pre-sorts it into **Gold** (High-Value), **Trash** (Suboptimal), and **Glitch** (Hallucinations).
3.  **The Strategic Brief:** The PSC presents a condensed "Workshop Summary" to the Architect, highlighting conflicts and suggesting specific hardenings.
4.  **Architect Authorization:** The Architect reviews the brief and provides the "Write" command: what to implement, what to scrap, and what to tweak.
5.  **The Re-Forge:** The PSC generates the **vNext** draft and attaches a **Changelog of Intent** (logging *why* changes were made) before returning to Phase 2 or exiting the loop.

### Phase 4: Implementation (The Green Light)
The Architect exits the loop once **Stochastic Parity** is reached—the state where adversarial nodes stop identifying new structural flaws.

## 3. Architecture: The Anchor & The Forge

### 3.1 Primary State-Controller (PSC) - The Partner-in-State
The PSC is the **Architect's Right Hand**. It is a non-autonomous advisor that maintains the "Global State," bridges the technical knowledge gap for the user, and flags contradictions. It ensures the **Will** of the Architect is never compromised by stochastic noise.

### 3.2 Individualized Recursive Memory (IRM) - The Forge
The Forge consists of rotating high-echelon LLMs with private threads. The **Changelog of Intent** ensures that the Forge never goes in circles by providing "Institutional Memory" of previous decisions.
